.About Orthopaedic Studio


Orthopaedic Studio is developed by Dr. Carl Siversson who is a researcher at the department of Medical Radiation Physics at Lund University in Sweden and also head of the software development team at Spectronic AB. The functionality of Orthopaedic Studio was designed in collaboration with the orthopaedic specialists at Children’s Hospital at Harvard Medical School in Boston.

Carl is responsible for maintaining and updating the software. If you have any suggestions, bug reports or questions you are welcome to contact him directly.

.About Spectronic


Spectronic AB is situated in Helsingborg, Sweden and is engaged in research and development of electronics and software for telecommunications and medical applications. The company have previously developed and manufactured a series of advanced mobile telephones, but is today also developing software for medical imaging analysis.
